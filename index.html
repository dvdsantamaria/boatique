Services we offer

Yacht Consulting

Every vessel owner has unique goals, and we help turn those goals into reality. With the right advice, we save you time and simplify the complex process of purchasing, maintaining or selling a vessel—covering logistics, legal, and compliance. Our consulting services include owner representation, maritime surveys, legal support, pre-purchase inspections, valuation studies, project management, safety consultation, import/export logistics, yacht transport, maintenance program setup and crew sourcing.

Filippetti Yachts Ambassador

Sales and commercial contact for the Australian region

We are the direct contact for the Filippetti Yachts Shipyard in Mondolfo, Italy, supporting owners, agents and buyers in Australia. With a history of importing, maintaining and managing Filippetti vessels locally, we handle sales, warranty support, maintenance programs and part sourcing. Founded in 2009 and rooted in over 40 years of Italian superyacht building, the Filippetti yard blends luxury craftsmanship, performance, and customisation, with award-winning designers working closely with clients to bring their nautical visions to life.
