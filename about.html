A bit about us

Boatique Marine brings together extensive knowledge and contacts that can only be gained through decades of experience, dating back to the America's Cup held in Fremantle, Western Australia, in 1987. Our journey in the yachting world has been built on firsthand exposure to the industry's evolution, and our roots are deeply embedded in both local and international maritime culture.

Our team's expertise spans on-water qualifications and practical experience as Captains, Engineers, and Consultants, having worked extensively with sailing and motor vessels ranging in sizes from 15m to 90m. We've operated across Europe, the Caribbean, Asia, the South Pacific, and Australia crossing major oceans, managing long-range cruising, and attending prestigious boat shows such as Monaco, Cannes, and Fort Lauderdale as buyers' representatives. Along the way, we've built solid relationships with build yards, owners, and brokers, and developed a deep understanding of the complex relationship between a yacht and its owner.

Boatique Marine is a yacht specialist dedicated to facilitating luxury superyacht lifestyles and solutions. We connect owners, buyers, sellers, build yards, and brokers across the globe, offering bespoke yacht management and maintenance services tailored to the highest standards of discretion and detail. Whether you seek advice on your current vessel, plan to purchase, or simply need insights into the industry, we're here to help—and if we don't have the answer, we'll connect you with someone who does. Reach out to tap into our passion and expertise for all things yachting.
